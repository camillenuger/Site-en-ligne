FRANKENTYPE
Creation of a typography by hand from existing typographies and cut in newspapers. Work based on a sentence from Frankenstein's book, "I had never seen anything that was so destroyed," this phrase evokes a storm. It takes place around Dr. Frankenstein at the moment he creates the monster. The calm settles down, everything becomes vague, it is dark and everything seems unstable.
