Proposal for coverage for the APACOM 2019 directory
For this new edition of the directory, I decided to work on the diversity of professional members and the values ​​that this directory embodies: communication, openness, diversity and the evolution of trades. In order to create a coverage with the greatest impact and dynamism possible, I worked on the concept of multiple windows. Indeed, the windows reflect openness to the world around us and openness to others. And if, obviously, it is impossible to include a particular person or even a professional field, I chose to represent them with caricature characters. For this, I wanted to highlight different expressions of face from geometric shapes, playing with the reasons to discuss the diversity of trades. In addition, digital frames emphasize the reference to evolution and modernity.
